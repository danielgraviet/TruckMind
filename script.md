---
  TruckMind Demo Script (~2:45)

  ---
  [0:00–0:20] Hook

  ▎ "Starting a food truck is a big gamble. You don't know your menu, your customers, or your market until it's too late. TruckMind gives you a simulation before you spend a dime."

  Show the landing page / concept input field.

  ---
  [0:20–0:45] Step 1 — Pick Your Concept

  ▎ "It starts with a single sentence. I'm going to open a taco truck near BYU campus in Provo, Utah."

  Type the concept. Hit submit.

  ▎ "The Strategist agent immediately generates three business plans — value, premium, and niche — each with a full menu and pricing."

  Show the three strategy cards appearing.

  ---
  [0:45–1:15] Step 2 — Meet Your Market

  ▎ "Now it builds your customer base. Using real US Census data for Provo, the system generates 100 synthetic personas — college students, young families, professionals. Each one has an income, dietary
  restrictions, price sensitivity, and a backstory."

  Show personas populating the grid — avatars with names, ages, occupations.

  ▎ "These aren't fake names. They're statistically grounded representations of who actually lives near your truck."

  ---
  [1:15–1:50] Step 3 — Run the Simulation

  ▎ "Each strategy gets tested against those 100 personas. The AI interviews them — would they visit? What would they order? What's the max they'd pay?"

  Show the reaction board animating — sentiment emojis appearing, some excited, some negative.

  ▎ "We can see Comet Tacos is hitting 75% interest. Students love the price point but are pushing back on the al pastor burrito. The premium concept looks good on paper but only 60% would actually show up."

  Highlight the winning strategy card with stats: interest rate, projected daily revenue.

  ▎ "The system picks a winner — Comet Tacos, $85 in projected daily revenue — and explains why."

  ---
  [1:50–2:20] Step 4 — Launch the Business

  ▎ “Once a winner is chosen by the agent it proceeds to setup your shop”

   App navigates to the shop dashboard

  ▎ "This is your operations center. Real-time inventory, revenue counter, and an AI cashier taking orders."

  Show the ShopPage: inventory bars at full, $0 revenue, $500 cash.

  ▎ "Let's simulate a lunch rush."

  Click "Simulate Rush." Orders flood in. Metrics animate upward.

  ---
  [2:20–2:45] Step 5 — Autonomous Operations

  ▎ "Watch the action feed. The AI isn't just taking orders — it's running the business."

  Point to the ActionFeed as entries appear:

  ▎ "Street tacos just hit 5 orders — automatic 7% price bump. Horchata is running low — restock decision made. The al pastor burrito hasn't sold in 10 orders — 20% discount applied automatically."

  Show inventory bars turning yellow, then replenishing.

  ▎ "This is TruckMind. From concept to a simulated, operating food truck — in under 3 minutes. Imagine what it can do for your next business idea."
