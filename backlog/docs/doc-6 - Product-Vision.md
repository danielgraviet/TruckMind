# TruckMind — Product Vision

## One-Liner
An AI agent that autonomously operates a food truck business — from market research to live operations — in under 5 minutes.

## The Problem
Early-stage founders waste weeks guessing at product-market fit. They pick menus by intuition, set prices from competitor Google searches, and have no validation that anyone actually wants what they're selling. Real market research costs $10K+ and takes months. Most startups skip it entirely and learn the hard way.

## The Solution
TruckMind compresses the entire cycle — research, strategy, validation, launch, operation — into minutes. User types one sentence describing a food truck concept. The AI does everything else.

## How It Works (4 Layers)

**Layer 1 — Market Intelligence**
Pulls real US Census demographics for the target location. Income, age, education, household composition. Every subsequent decision is grounded in actual population data.

**Layer 2 — Silicon Sampling**
Generates 200-400 synthetic customer personas from Census data. Each has a name, age, income, dietary preferences, price sensitivity, flavor profile. This is the AI's test market — a synthetic population it can query before making any real decision.

**Layer 3 — Strategy & Simulation**
AI designs a complete business strategy (menu, pricing, positioning). Then stress-tests it against the synthetic population. Personas react individually — excited, neutral, hostile. AI refines strategy based on feedback.

**Layer 4 — Live Operations**
Shop opens. AI handles customer orders conversationally. Dynamic pricing adjusts in real-time based on demand. Inventory management with autonomous restocking. Surge response. All decisions are autonomous — zero human intervention after concept input.

## Why It Wins

**Autonomy (40% of score):** After one sentence of input, the AI makes every decision: menu design, pricing, inventory levels, restocking, customer service, price adjustments, item removal. Multi-agent architecture with 4 distinct autonomous roles.

**Business Value (30%):** Solves a real problem real founders have. Instant, data-grounded market validation that would otherwise cost $10K+ and take months. Utah startup ecosystem context is perfect for Podium judges.

**Technical Complexity (20%):** Multi-agent orchestration. Two-tier simulation (50 LLM + 250 rule-based). Census demographic grounding. Dynamic pricing engine. Conversational order management. Refinement loops.

**Demo Quality (10%):** Reaction board with 300 persona cards flipping to sentiment colors is visually compelling. Judge can interact with the cashier. Rush simulation shows autonomous decisions firing in real-time. Clear narrative arc: one sentence → operating business.

## The Secret Weapon: Silicon Sampling
Most AI demos generate random synthetic data. TruckMind builds a statistically grounded synthetic market from real Census demographics. This is how the best companies actually operate — TruckMind does it in seconds, and the AI tests its own strategy against this market before making a single real decision.

## The Closing Line
> Most hackathon projects build an AI that gives advice. TruckMind *is* the business.
